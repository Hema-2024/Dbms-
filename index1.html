Blood Management society is a page where users can check availabilty of blood at different hospitals. Every year 3million people die due to shortage of blood. We as a creators of this page feel this is due to lack of communication. That is why we created this page where users can register as donors , check for blood availabilty and gain knowledge about different blood types. Our Motto is "LIVE LET LIVE". Thank you for visiting our page.
